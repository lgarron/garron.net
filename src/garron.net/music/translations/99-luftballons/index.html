So generals began the chase
Sent fighter squadrons in the air
To sound alarms for when and where
But out there flying all long
Were neundneunzig Luftballons

Neundneunzig jets took flight

Thought they all were Cap'n Kirk
But ended up as fireworks
The neighbors heard a giant crack
And thought they were under attack
But what they shot at all long
Were neundneunzig Luftballons

Neundneunzig war ministers
Matchsticks next to fuel canisters
Thought they all were clever folk
And smelled their triumph in the smoke
They shouted "War!" and took their shot
Man, who ever would have thought
That all of this could ever come
from neundneunzig Luftballons?
